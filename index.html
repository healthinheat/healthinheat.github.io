ABOUT US

Key features of our organization to #FightHeat.

Community Health Partnerships

We aim to work with local clinics and healthcare facilities in Boston, MA and surrounding areas to provide necessary supplies for heat stress response and climate adaptation.

Advocacy and Health Policy

Advocating for legislation that supports climate change and works to address the health-related challenges of global warming and climate change.

Education

Working to educate wide audiences including students, teachers, healthcare providers, and more using effective climate communication and educating on tangible steps for prepardness and prevention.

Climate and Health Equity Mission

The core of our work is centered on a mission of equitable healthcare delivery, education, and access to safety from heat and other climate-related changes in the years to come.
